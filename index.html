This is Zoutkeet

Embark on a unique journey with Rick Pels, a dedicated guardian of Amsterdam's canals in the enchanting district of Westelijke Eilanden. Here, the spirit of one individual meets the water's edge, weaving a narrative of passion, commitment, and a solo mission for cleaner, more pristine canals.

In the heart of Westelijke Eilanden, Around Zoutkeetsgracht, Rick Pels has taken it upon himself to be the defender of the iconic canals that define the character of these historic islands. His love for shipfaring makes this a somewhat easier task to complete in his own accord. But he might need the help of you! The kind volunteers.

Be part of a compelling story of the one-man-army Rick Pels, trying to make an impact on the cleanliness of Westelijke Eilanden. Sadly Rick cant do it all on his own, he needs help and so does the Zoutkeetsgracht. We must preserve the beauty of this precious canal!
